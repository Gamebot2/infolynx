Easy note taking

With InfoLynx, you'll never again have to switch tabs between your video lecture and your note-taking window. For your convenience, you can simply play your video and take notes directly in our note window, and download those notes as a text file whenever you like!

Reference Window

InfoLynx will also automatically pull up a list of keywords from the video you enter, so that you'll never have to look up a word while watching a lecture again! It places the term, an image, and a wikipedia link in our handy dandy reference window, which you can easily add to your notes at the push of a button.
